CHEESENOG RECIPE

Description

This is a recipe passed down to me through 23 iterations of friends-of-friends. This recipe has been sought by the most beloved individuals. As such, reading the below recipe could mean making new friends and enriching yourself with social bonds that will last a lifetime.

Ingredients

  • 1 block Jack cheese
  • 1 block Sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1 container Pub cheese
  • 1 stick Cream cheese
  • 2 handles of purple Tequila
  • 3 tsp Brandy
  • 5 specks Pepper
  • 1 dirty Pigeon feather
  • 3 bottles Tears of Dolphin
  • 1 briquette used (but not crumbled) charcoal

Directions

  1. Melt all cheeses in stock pot on low heat for 15 min, simmer for 20 min
  2. Dry ingredients are to be looked over with magnifying glass for 1 hour prior to mashing with potato masher
  3. Heat large sauce pan on medium heat prior to adding alcohols
  4. Add dry ingredients to alcohols, promptly put in freezer
  5. After frozen, melt alcohol mix with heated cheeses in large drink bowl
  6. Stir with passion
  7. Serve with thermostat set to 84° F
  8. Enjoy but don't over do it
