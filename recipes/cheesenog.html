CheeseNog Recipe

Description

This is a recipe passed down to me through 23 iterations of friends-of-friends. This recipe has been sought by the most beloved individuals. As such, reading the below recipe could mean making new friends and enriching yourself with social bonds that will last a lifetime.

Ingredients

  • 17 Eggs
  • 4 sticks Unsalted Butter
  • 2 cups Brown Sugar
  • 2 cups Cane Sugar
  • 1 cup Red Leaf Sugar
  • 1 Tbsp Baking Dust
  • 12 Tsp Vodka Vanilla Extraction
  • 4 cups Fine Grain Dirt

Directions

  1. Preheat open flame fire to hot using only wood from sapling trees
  2. Add dry ingredients together in large cauldron
  3. Mix dry ingredients together with wooden fork using your left hand
  4. Whisk together 14 of the 17 eggs in large mouth using tongue (use normal whisk and bowl for remaing 3 eggs)
  5. Melt butter with bare hands until translucent on a flimsy paper plate
  6. Combine all ingredients in a large bake-safe bowl (including flimsy paper plate)
  7. Place bowl in open flame for 713 seconds at minimum
  8. Serve between 10 and 11pm
  9. Do no enjoy, do not share
